Design Details

Teal Palette Used

  • ■ #2dd4bf — 1st place accent & highlights
  • ■ #0d9488 — 1st podium gradient, avatars, button
  • ■ #14b8a6 — 2nd place border
  • ■ #0f766e — 2nd/3rd podium gradient
  • ■ #115e59 — 3rd podium, subtle accents
  • ■ #5eead4 — Rank numbers, score text

Edge Cases

  • 2 participants: podium shows 1st + 2nd only (no 3rd column)
  • 3 participants: full podium, no ranked list below
  • 1 team in team mode: single centered 1st place column
  • Participant avatars use deterministic colors from getAvatarColor()
